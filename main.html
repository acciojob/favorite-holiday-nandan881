Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its stunning turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater bungalows. It's an ideal getaway for relaxation, water sports, and exploring vibrant marine life.

  1. Maafushi Island - A blend of local culture and scenic beaches.
  2. Banana Reef - A famous diving spot with vibrant coral reefs.
  3. Male City - The bustling capital with rich history and landmarks.
  4. The Glow-In-The-Dark Beach - Experience the natural phenomenon of bioluminescent waves.
  5. Hulhumale - A picturesque artificial island with modern vibes.
  • Mas Huni - A traditional Maldivian breakfast made with tuna, coconut, and onion.
  • Garudhiya - A flavorful fish soup served with rice, lime, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas - Grilled fish marinated with spices.
  • Boshi Mashuni - A banana flower salad with a unique taste.
  • Maldivian Curry - A rich and spicy curry featuring fresh seafood.
